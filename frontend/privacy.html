Privacy Policy

At Online Examination System, protecting the privacy of our users is one of our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy document explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard your data while you use our platform.

This Privacy Policy applies only to activities conducted through this website and does not apply to information collected offline or through other channels.

Consent

By accessing or using the Online Examination System, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information We Collect

We collect information only when it is necessary to provide and improve our services. The types of information we may collect include:

  • User role information (Admin or User)
  • Exam participation data such as attempts, scores, and progress
  • Exam content uploaded by administrators
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and access timestamps

Personal details are collected only when explicitly provided by the user, for example during authentication or system interactions.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To operate, manage, and maintain online examinations
  • To analyze exam performance and generate analytics
  • To improve system security and reliability
  • To enhance user experience and platform functionality
Log Files

The Online Examination System follows standard practices of using log files. These logs may include IP addresses, browser types, Internet Service Providers (ISP), date and time stamps, and page navigation data. This information is used solely for system administration, analytics, and security monitoring.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to store user preferences and session information. Cookies help us improve user experience and ensure smooth platform operation. You may disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Exam data and system records are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized users.

Third-Party Services

The Online Examination System does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. Any third-party services used are strictly limited to system operation and infrastructure support.

Children’s Information

Protecting children’s privacy is important to us. The Online Examination System does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such information is discovered, we will take immediate steps to remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately upon publication.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support options provided on this website.
